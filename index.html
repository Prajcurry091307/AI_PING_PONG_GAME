AI PING PONG GAME

Instructions

  1. First , keep your Laptop/Computer screen straight
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the Laptop/Computer
  3. Move your Right Wrist in an ascending and descending manner, A red dot should appear on your right wrist .
  4. Now, press the play button , And again move yourself 3-4 feet away from Laptop/Computer
  5. The Red Paddel is your paddel , that will move as per the movement of your right wrist .
